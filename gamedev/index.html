Frequently Asked Questions

What are you working on?

I'm working on individual Capcom characters for MUGEN so I can solidify my development framework and process, as well as Project TITAN as my next commercial title.

What about your MUGEN projects?

For now I'm focused in working on individual Capcom characters. Expect some news from time to time; I'd love to finish my personal vision for Capcom Fighting All-Stars, so you can expect news about it in the future too!

What is your vision for your future characters?

Versus series gameplay inspired by the Marvel Vs. Capcom saga, that can allow me to create a stable development platform to release more content as time goes on. Shuma-Gorath is right now my base for future developments.

Where can I get more information about your commercial games?

BeWolfStudio.com - It's a one-man indie game development studio (me) with a vision focused on storytelling, visual atmosphere and captivating gameplay. It's a small project right now, and I want to make it sustainable in the long run.
