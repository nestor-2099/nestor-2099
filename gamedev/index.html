FAQ

Frequently Asked Questions

Are you still working on Capcom Fighting All-Stars (CFAS)?

Yes. As time went on, through the past years I've learned more so I can improve my design and development processes, for both my hobbies and my professional career.

CFAS has a special place in my heart, not only for the game itself, but for all the incredible people I knew, I know and I may meet in the future. I hope I can eventually finish this game for all of them, for the great friends who have helped me after all these years to achieve my vision.

How are you planning to work on CFAS?

First, finish my current char WIP's related to the first demo, which includes Batsu and Fiona. After that, set all the systems (Screenpack, lifebars, announcers...) in an unified package.

The roster was reduced in order to get a complete version of the game released once and for all.

What about Marvel Vs. Capcom UDB?

For now, CFAS will receive my attention; I may work on some of the Marvel spots as standalone releases meanwhile. One big project at a time.

What is your vision for CFAS?

Versus series tag team game, inspired by the Marvel Vs. Capcom saga, starting with a 2vs2 system that I'd like to evolve into a 3v3 system. A stable platform which will receive updates as time goes on.

Who is included in CFAS?

  • Ryu [Street Fighter 2]
  • Fiona [Haunting Ground]
  • Morrigan [Darkstalkers]
  • Batsu [Rival Schools]
  • More to come!

The focus is on the diversity of Capcom's franchises for the first phase of the project.

Can you include X/Y/Z in the project?

There are a lot of characters I'd like to add, but that will be possible only with a stable platform, which is what this project is aiming for.

Where can I get more information about your commercial games?

BeWolfStudio.com - It's a one-man indie game development studio (me) with a vision focused on storytelling, visual atmosphere and captivating gameplay. It's a small project right now, and I want to make it sustainable in the long run.

How can I help you?

Your comments, feedback and support is always a great help! You can support me at Patreon, where you can get exclusive perks like betas of my MUGEN content and free copies of my games.

All my MUGEN works are free as always; your support in Patreon will help me to focus more time coding all the content above. I'll work hard to release polished videogames and contributions which I hope you'll like.
